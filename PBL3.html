PBL 3 Elements

Barrow Island Act - Production, Conservation & Sustainability

Knowing the process of extracting the resources and understanding the carbon dioxide injection project

During #PBL3, OysterCatcher had to lose one member, Theddy Langsa. We had to work with only 4 people in the group. Thankfully, not long after #PBL3 started, we received a new member, Jordan Mollenoyux.

#PBL3 is about the extraction and transportation of the Liquified Natural Gas (LNG) and the process taken afterm called the Carbon Dioxide Injection. Our project for this PBL was to:

  • Conduct an investigation on Corrosion and Carbon Capture Storage
  • Engineer and create an animation of the Gorgon sub-sea model
  • Advertise and apply for 2 jobs
