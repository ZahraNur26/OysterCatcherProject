Understanding why is Barrow Island classified as A Class Reserve and knowing the biological threat into its ecosystem.

During this PBL, the main concept was Human Biology, we were required to integrate and corrolate the three Investigations to Barrow Island. The Investigation were;

  • Workforce Diversity
  • Quarantine and Environmental Management
  • OHS & Safety
We were also required to build an Environmental Management System along with an App for Investigation 2 and also an app for Investigation 3. Celebrating Barrow Island is also another element of this PBL which you can also check it out.

Investigation 1 - Workforce Diversity

The purpose of this investigation was to examine the diversity that exists within a working environment, this refers to the variety between people such as race, genfer, ethnic group, age, personality in lifestyle and background.

Investigation 2 - Quarantine Environmental Management

The purpose of this investigation was to understand how human activity can affect the quality of water and how it relates to Barrow Island, the key concepts of this investigation was mutation, evolution and natural selection.
