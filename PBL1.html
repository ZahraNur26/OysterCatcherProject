Chevron - Energy - Human & Powering the Earth

Introducing Liquified Natural Gas (LNG)

During this PBL, we were first introduced into LNG and Barrow Island as a Class A reserve. Aside from the new topic that we were introduced into, we were also introduced into a new way of learning, which is the PBL (Project Based Learning), in our PBLs, we are free to do what we wish and let our imagination and creativity develop and grow to do something that we haven't done before in our schooling years. The PBL 1 Elements are as folow:

  • Learning Map Interpretation
  • Bridges Investigation
  • Importance of Energy in Today's Society (Awareness Commercial)
  • OSPREY Card

Each of these elements can be seen in our #PBL1 website
